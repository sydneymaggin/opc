How to Cut Your Own Hair

*Non-Foolproof Tutorial*

1. Go to the store and get haircutting scissors

2. Look online for what type of hairstyle you want

3. Go onto youtube and watch a bunch of videos of people giving that haircut

4. Section out your hair into small parts

5. Start making small snips, and always go longer than you think you want

6. Cut until you are satisfied, or until you need to get your friend for damage control
